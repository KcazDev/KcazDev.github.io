About Me

Who I Am

2+ Years Building
6+ Projects

I'm a developer and digital creator at Boise State University, studying Games, Interactive Media, and Mobile (GIMM) a discipline that sits at the intersection of technology, design, and storytelling. Games didn't just shape my childhood, they shaped the way I think. Where most people saw entertainment, I saw systems to understand, mechanics to dissect, and worlds worth rebuilding from the ground up. That curiosity drove me into development and hasn't let go since. Today I build across the full stack, from immersive 3D environments in Unity and Unreal Engine to responsive web apps powered by Node.js and MySQL. I pair that engineering foundation with hands-on digital art skills in Photoshop and Adobe Animate, giving me the rare ability to own both the code and the creative vision behind a project.

My goal is to use my skills to push the world forward. Whether through games that challenge how we feel, tools that redefine how we work, or systems that change how people interact with technology altogether. I'm not interested in building things that blend in. I want to build things that matter.
