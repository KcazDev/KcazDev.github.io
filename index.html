About Me

I'm Zackery Lake a developer at Boise State University majoring in GIMM. I specialize in Unity (C#), Unreal Engine (C++/Blueprints), and full stack development with HTML, JavaScript, and Node.js. Over the last two years, I've built projects that reflect not just my technical ability, but my drive to constantly improve. I've always believed in the power of creating something incredible from nothing. Games shaped my childhood, but instead of stopping at playing them, I wanted to understand how they were built. That mindset pushed me into development and continues to fuel my work today. As I've advanced in my studies, I've realized that interactive technology has the potential to transform entire industries. My goal is to use my skills to help push the world forward whether that's through games, tools, or systems that redefine how people interact with technology.
