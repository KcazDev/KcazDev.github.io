I'm Zackery Lake — a developer at Boise State University majoring in GIMM. I specialize in Unity (C#), Unreal Engine (C++/Blueprints), and full-stack development with HTML, JavaScript, and Node.js.

Games shaped my childhood, but instead of stopping at playing them, I wanted to understand how they were built. That mindset pushed me into development and continues to fuel my work today.

My goal is to use my skills to push the world forward — whether through games, tools, or systems that redefine how people interact with technology.
