Knights Adventure

Knights Adventure is a 2D platformer built in Unity using C#, where players take on the role of a brave knight on a quest to reclaim the crown and restore the kingdom. Every asset in the game was designed and created from scratch, making it a fully original experience from the ground up. Key features include a dynamic parallax background that brings the world to life with a sense of depth and movement, fluid character animations that react to the player's actions, and a fully programmed jumping mechanic and death sequence that give the game a polished, responsive feel. This project holds a special place as my very first complete game — and the one that sparked my passion for programming. Through countless late nights of debugging and problem-solving, I gained hands-on experience with Unity's 2D engine, animation systems, game physics, and core gameplay programming. Finishing Knights Adventure taught me more than any tutorial could, and crossing that finish line gave me a deep sense of accomplishment that pushed me to keep building.
